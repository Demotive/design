Call 999 now if you:

  • have sudden, bad stomach pain, especially if it’s in one place
  • are vomiting blood
  • have bloody stool
  • are in pain when you touch your stomach

You need help now because it could be something serious like appendicitis or a stomach ulcer.

Rash with no other symptoms

Small white spots (milia) often appear on a baby’s face when they are a few days old. They usually clear up within a few weeks and don’t need treatment.
